"All those moments will be lost in time... like… tears in rain"

The movie Blade Runner holds a special and iconic position in the history of science fiction cinema, signifying the dawn of a new cinematic era during the early 1980s. This dark and dystopian world where the story unfolds has become the epitome of cyberpunk, a subgenre of science fiction that has evolved over the years to become a major source of inspiration for countless artists, writers, directors, and video game creators. The Blade Runner universe is a haunting portrayal of the future, where advanced technology exists alongside a corrupt society, and it's a world that prompts us to ponder the enduring impact it has had on pop culture and our contemplation of technological advancements and the ethical dilemmas that accompany progress.
